PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: April 2026

1. Summary

Omnithrix does not collect, store, or process personal data. We do not operate user accounts, logins, or databases of user information.

2. What We Don't Collect

  • Names, emails, phone numbers
  • IP addresses or browsing history
  • Cookies or tracking pixels on this site
  • Analytics data from this site

3. Third-Party Platforms

When you watch our videos on YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, or similar platforms, those platforms collect data according to their own privacy policies. We receive aggregate, anonymized analytics from these platforms (view counts, like rates, demographics) — not individual user data.

4. Comments & Interactions

Comments you leave on our videos are subject to the platform's terms (YouTube, TikTok, etc.). We may read these comments to improve content but do not extract personal information from them.

5. Children's Privacy

Our content is appropriate for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.

6. Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above.

7. Contact

Questions? Reach out via our channel pages on the respective platforms.
